Privacy Policy for Movvie

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Movvie ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our movie discovery application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Movvie does not require account creation, email addresses, names, or any personally identifiable information to use the app.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your movie shortlist (saved movies)
  • Your swipe history (movies you've seen)
  • App preferences (filters, settings)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Movvie uses the following third-party service:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) - We retrieve movie information, posters, ratings, and trailers from TMDB's public API. When you browse movies, your device makes requests to TMDB's servers. Please review TMDB's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

External Links

Movvie may contain links to external websites (such as JustWatch for streaming availability or YouTube for trailers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Children's Privacy

Movvie is designed to be family-friendly and includes content filtering for children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset Everything" option in the app's Settings
  • Deleting the Movvie app from your device

Analytics and Tracking

Movvie does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
